Step 2: Connecting Sensors to the Network

Follow these steps to connect your IoT sensors to a reliable network for real-time data transmission:

  1. Identify the nearest Wi-Fi or cellular network available on the farm.
  2. Power up the IoT device and access the configuration settings.
  3. Input the network credentials (SSID and password) into the device settings.
  4. Test the connection to ensure data is being sent to the cloud or local storage.
