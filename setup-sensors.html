Step 1: Setting Up IoT Sensors

Follow these steps to set up soil moisture and temperature sensors for your farm:

  1. Unpack the sensors and read the user manual for initial setup.
  2. Place the sensors in the field at appropriate depths (e.g., 5-10 cm for soil moisture).
  3. Connect the sensors to a microcontroller or gateway device for data transmission.
  4. Test the sensors to ensure accurate data collection.
